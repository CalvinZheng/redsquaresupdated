How to Minimize Noise Interference

If you detect jitter in tracker measurements, you may be operating in a workspace in which noise in the 60Hz bandwidth is present. One way of dealing with this noise is to change DC tracker filter settings. These settings are adjustable to let you control the type and amount of filtering to be applied to your measurements. 

If you need to reduce noise to a minimum, consider employing our default filters: a wide band notch AC filter and an adaptive DC filter. These filters will squelch noise but should be employed judiciously to minimize lag in outputs. Another good option is to employ a narrow AC notch filter, which does not have much effect on dynamic performance. See you users manual or contact techsupport@ascension-tech.com for more information about changing filter settings to reduce tracker noise.
