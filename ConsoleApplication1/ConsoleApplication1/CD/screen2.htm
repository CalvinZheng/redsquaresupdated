Optimizing Tracker Performance

For best performance, be sure to take advantage of our toolbox of tracker aids. 

Your DC tracker will operate as specified for many years to come, provided you take a few straightforward steps to minimize metallic distortion (inaccurate measurements) and environmental noise (jittery data). 

Some steps are as simple as changing your measurement rate or not mounting the transmitter / sensors on or too close to metallic platforms or objects. Others may require you to change parameter settings, use the metal detector included in this software utility, or use our Environmental Analyzer.
